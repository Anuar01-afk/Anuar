    Game Mechanics

    Weapons and ballisticsFiring modes:
    Automatic, single or burst fire. The choice depends on the distance. Bullet physics: Projectiles in the game are subject to the laws of gravity, so at medium and long distances you need to take a “lead” and take into account the reduction in trajectory. Recoil: Each barrel has a unique recoil pattern, which must be compensated by moving your finger down while shooting.

    Sanhok Map Guide & Overview (PUBG)Sanhok is a tropical jungle map in PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS, featuring lush vegetation, ancient ruins, and military facilities.     Karta Karakin

    Karakin is a compact, high-tension 2×2 km desert island. It is known for its octopus farming, textile trade, and as the homeland of Sadiya (the Battlebride). Supporting up to 64 players, the map combines the open-field tension of Miramar with the rapid pacing of Sanhok. There is no water on the map—it is purely landmass and rocks.
